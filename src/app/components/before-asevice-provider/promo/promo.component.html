How it works

Only the best sitters and dog walkers get listed on Minderz. If you have personal or professional experience watching pets or walking dogs, you could be a great fit.


Set up your profile.

Tell us what makes you a standout sitter and include details of your pet sitting or dog walking experience.

Organise your Calendar.

Choose your services, rates, and block out the days you're unavailable, eg. going on holiday.

Add Photos

Adding photos that portray you as an animal lover with a safe home. Having a clear professional profile image is key as this will display as a thumbnail across the site.

Respond to jobs & enquiries!

Pet Owners will search their local area for Sitters and inquire with you directly.
